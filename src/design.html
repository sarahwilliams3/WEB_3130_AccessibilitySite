Site Design Process

Visually this site was built to be very simple. The color scheme was chosen to work together, but still be visully readable when overlaid. These colors show my love for darker colors mixed with a lighter color to accent. There is also enough white space to help everything be readable with out having too much white space. To chose the color scheme I used adobe color. Visually I want this site to be simple since it is more of an informative page. Despite its simplicity it shows the use of visual design being accessible. The visual design of this site will meet WCAG2.1 AA guidelines.

The structural design is also very simple, but accessible. I used level one headings with level two headings following that and so forth. I want the structure to make sense for anyone using a screen reader, but also to be visually appealing and accessible to others as well. The nav is simple and easy to use while the rest of the page is easy to follow because heading levels are organized. The language used in this site is plain enough for all to understand. The Structural Design of this site will meet WCAG2.1 AA guidelines.
